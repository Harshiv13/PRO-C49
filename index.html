This website is about making origami using animals.

Here are some facts about two animals, with instructions on how to build origami versions of them:

Interesting facts about chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting facts about pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intellegent animals.
  2. They are renowned for their exceptional navigational abilities.
  3. They also have excellent hearing abilities.
